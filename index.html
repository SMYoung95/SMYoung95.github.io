Greetings Traveler!
May I interest you in, my portfolio?

Hi there, I'm Sebastian M. Young, a junior game designer with a passion for crafting immersive and enjoyable gameplay experiences. Over the past year, I've had the privilege of working on several exciting game projects in various roles, including Level Designer and System Designer. Additionally, I've contributed my skills to the art department as a 3D Modeller and Landscape Artist, as well as taking on technical roles in Programming, Shader Design, Quality Assurance, Testing, and Evaluation. I'm always looking for new opportunities to expand my skills and create innovative games that captivate and entertain players.

On this portfolio, you'll find some examples of the different games I've worked on throughout my career, as well as some personal projects that showcase my skills and creativity. I'm always pushing myself to learn new things and take on new challenges, and I believe in collaborative discourse and teamwork to make the best games possible.

Feel free to take a look at my work and contact me if you have any questions or would like to discuss a potential project. Thank you for visiting my portfolio!
