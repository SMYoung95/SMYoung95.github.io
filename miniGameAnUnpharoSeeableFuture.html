Gam Jam: An Unpharo Seeable Future (2019)

For An Unpharo Seeable Future functioned as the System Designer and the 2D Character Artist, my tasks include:

  • Design the different characters
  • Animate all characters
  • Planning of how the combat system should be designed.

I had only recently began to utlize photoshop, so it was quite a challenge for me at the time to design and create the art for the different characters, all which can be seen below.

The output of the first Ludum Dare Game Jam I participated in with my friends.
Story of a pharaoh who performs a dark ritual, in his death to ascend to godhood. The player is a young man affected by this ritual, and in his grief makes a pact with the god Osiris who is disgusted by the perversion which gains him access to the otherworld and a tremendous power ( A magic Ankh ). He uses this power to hunt the soul of the pharaoh and banish it before it can achieve godhood.
An egyptian themed dungeon crawler with five descending levels of afterlife that the player goes through fighting spirits. Can you fight your way through and defeat the Pharaoh!
Objective of the game is to pass through each level, unlocking new powers and defeating different types monsters inspired by Egyptian mythology. Game can be found and played for free Here
